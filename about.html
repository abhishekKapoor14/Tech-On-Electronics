About us

Established in the year 2015 at Noida (Uttar Pradesh, India), we, "Tech On Electronics", are counted as the reliable manufacturer and supplier of a broad array of SMT PCB Assembly, LED Lights, PCB Assembly, LED Drivers and many more products.
These products are designed by our assiduous professionals using quality tested material and modern technology in tune with the set industry norms. Offered products are broadly distinguished for their exceptional features like quick installation, precision-designed, hassle free performance, long working life, durability and light weight. Our provided products are obtainable in varied specifications that suit the varied requirements of our honored clients.
